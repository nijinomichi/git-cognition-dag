# Observation 001

## Question

Are Recover and Reciprocity of Care equivalent?

## Observation

They are related but not identical.

Recover describes an action or transition.

Reciprocity of Care describes the relational field that permits recovery without extraction or domination.

## Question

Are Create and Creative Reciprocity equivalent?

## Observation

Create is an event.

Creative Reciprocity is the return and circulation of creative value.
